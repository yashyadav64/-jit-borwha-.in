About JNCET TRUST

JAWAHARLAL NEHRU CHARITABLE EDUCATIONAL TRUST, BORAWAN was founded in 1996 under the Trust Registration Act with the registration no.1/95-96 dated 13-06-1996 by Late Shri Subhash Ji Yadav a man with the vision to serve the community at large.

Since its inception the trust is striving hard for the development of poor & weaker section of the society viz, SC, ST, OBC & Minority. During the last 20 years trust had made deliberate efforts for the development of rural youth belonging to SC ST & OBC category by establishing various education Institutes to empower them in all aspects as financially & socially.

Our Facilities

Hostels

JIT has three separate hostels equipped with all modern facilities like reading room, recreation room, TV room, indoor and outdoor games with hygienic breakfast, lunch and dinner. These hostels for junior boys, senior boys and girls, in its own campus.

Our Infrastructure

Jawaharlal Institute of Technology as a hub of technical education, is situated at Borawan, Khargone. With a positive atmosphere it provides students an opportunity to sharpen skills and acquire positive attitude.
